Independent living

Through our hour-long independent living sessions, we aim to develop the full A-to-Z of self-sufficiency. Helping youths to reach their goals within education, employment, housing, health, mental health, transportation, financial intelligence, and basic life skills through an independent living transitional plan, we offer a fully comprehensive service.

A goal-oriented program, we use a range of approaches to help youths accomplish their objectives. With at least one session per week, we fuse a combination of youth-driven, evidence-based, and experiential learning to create our formalized program model, driven by the outcomes of the youths we partner with. Ultimately, we base our success on them, with tri-annual checks after the individual steps out of our doors.

Young Adult Voluntary Foster Care

Facilitating an ease into adulthood, our Young Adult Voluntary Foster Care (YAVFC) program allows under 21s to benefit from an extended foster care period. Whether they’re still gaining from independent living support or perhaps they’re simply not ready to fly the nest, we believe that the youth-to-young adult period should be a seamless one, where aging out is a process based on their terms.

Benefitting from financial stipends and case management, with a crutch in the form of experienced professionals at their fingertips, our voluntary foster care service acts as a safety net during the transition.

Counseling

At R.O.A.D to Success, we work to combat the trauma that often comes hand-in-hand with foster care. With a range of mental health diagnoses stemming from feelings of abandonment, neglect, and identity issues, we offer bespoke counseling centred on the needs of each individual.

Led by a team of specialists, our counseling services are founded on a goal to unlock brighter futures for the youths we work with, where life skills are learned, diagnoses are treated, and coping strategies are developed. Helping fostered youths to explore their behavioral patterns, reactions, and relationships as they prepare for adulthood, we offer a compassionate hand as they approach enhanced emotional wellbeing.
