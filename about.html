Resiliency. Optimism. Adversity. Destination:
The R.O.A.D to Success formula.

A Michigan-based independent living program, we specialize in supporting fostered youths as they embark on new beginnings. Our mission? To provide exceptional case management and therapeutic services, equipping older youths with the knowledge and skills to navigate through their next chapter.

To us, it’s so much more than a role. At our core lies you. With a youth-first approach, we focus on preparing you for your destination, tailoring our program to meet your preferences, experiences, and ultimately, the person that you want to become. Partnering with youths, we laser-focus on developing skills to help you thrive, from basic life skills through to financial intelligence and beyond.

Bridging the gap between youths and a fulfilled life, we integrate a golden blend of approaches for optimum results. Utilizing evidence-based practices and youth-driven activities, coupled with a multitude of therapeutic experiences, we target success from all angles, living by the adage “It takes a village to prepare a youth for adulthood.”

From the first chat to the final farewell, our highly experienced professionals are always on-hand with a listening ear, words of encouragement, and an arsenal of solutions in tow. The result? A ‘you’ that feels ready to take on the world beyond foster care. A ‘you’ that has the skills to lead a happy, healthy life. And a ‘you’ that is given the best start to adulthood, with a toolkit of LifeSet strategies.
