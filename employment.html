We’re hiring!

Do you want to contribute to guiding youths towards a path of success? Do you take pride in your listening ear and empathetic soul?

An exceptionally close-knit team, we are seeking compassionate, warm, and skilled professionals to seamlessly integrate into our workforce. With an emphasis on our youth-first approach, we strive to create a positive working environment, where your needs are nurtured and progression opportunities are plentiful.

If you’d like to join our growing team, we’d love to hear from you. The following roles are available:

Independent Living Transitional Specialist

A rewarding opportunity for an independent living specialist, we are seeking an experienced professional to join our growing team. Suitable for the holder of a bachelor or master’s degree in social work (or a related field), we require the following:

Working knowledge of child welfare policies

Awareness and respect of cultural differences

A demonstrable ability to build strong worker-youth relationship

Excellent verbal and written communication skills

Ability to pass a criminal and central registry check

Contractual Therapist

An opportunity to make a real difference, we are seeking a fully licensed therapist to join our team. An enriching role working hand-in-hand with a small caseload of 8-10, you will play an integral role in the empowerment of youths. Suitable for the holder of a master’s degree in social work (or a related field), we require the following::

1+ year experience working with children and families

Working knowledge of basic principles, concepts, and methodology of social work

Knowledgeable of the implications of trauma

Excellent verbal and written communication skills

Ability to pass a criminal and central registry check
